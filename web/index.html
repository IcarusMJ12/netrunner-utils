Click on a card name to add it to your deck. Right-click to remove.

Click on the card stats to the right of the card name to view additional card metadata. Right-click to collapse.

Click on the white deck bar (which appears when any card is added) for expanded deck view that contains additional options. Click again to collapse.

Any clickable item will become translucent on mouseover. Don't be afraid to experiment!
